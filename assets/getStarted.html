Step 1: Find a Registered Solar Panel Installer

Find verified, registered professionals to install your solar systems Here

Step 2: Apply for Rebate Program

Have your installer apply for the Solar Homes Rebate Program or the Greener Homes Grant Program for you.

Step 3: Wait for Confirmation

Wait for the rebate application to be approved and then go ahead with installation.

Step 4: Recieve your Rebate

It can take approximately 8 weeks to recieve your rebate.

These Products can be installed for free!

  • Water-saving Showerheads

LED Light Bulbs

ENS will convert every lightbulb in your home to an efficient LED lightbulb. These are proven to be less expensive to run, last longer than other inefficient lightbulbs, and is 90% more efficient.

Water-saving Showerheads

Water-saving shower heads can save you up to $100 per year if your households shower time equals 10 min/day. This product maintains water pressure to help you maintain your bank account.

Smart Thermostat

Smart thermostat for mini-split heat pumps can learn your routine to adjust your thermostat to just the right temperature. Studies have shown that users can save an average of 12% on the heating and cooling of their home.

Hot Water Tank Wrap

This product helps keep your heat tank warm for longer. This reduces the heat loss from your tank to your tap, saving about 4-9% on the water heating bill. Approximately saving you 35$/year.

Faucet Aerators

This is very similar to the water-saving showerheads, but for your faucets! This simple product screws onto your tap to maintain pressure and could save you $20/year.
